# Business Analysis Documentation Portfolio

## Overview

This repository contains a collection of Business Analysis deliverables created while practicing and applying Business Analysis methodologies across different business scenarios.

The objective of this portfolio is to demonstrate my ability to analyse business problems, document requirements, and create standard Business Analysis artefacts used throughout the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC).

---

## Documents Included

### Business Requirements Document (BRD)

Defines the business problem, project objectives, scope, business requirements and proposed solution for an online banking enhancement initiative.

---

### Functional Requirements Document (FRD)

Provides detailed functional requirements, business rules, validations, process flows, activity diagrams and wireframes for the proposed solution.

---

### Stakeholder Register

Identifies key stakeholders, their roles, influence, interests, communication preferences and level of impact to support effective stakeholder management.

---

### User Stories

Agile user stories written with acceptance criteria to capture business requirements from an end-user perspective.

---

### Use Case

Documents user interactions with the system, including actors, preconditions, normal flow, alternate flow and expected outcomes.
